John's Favorite Family Night Movies

The Movies I Enjoy That The Kids Can Watch too!

The Lego Movie

Really funny, goofy stuff. Great cast of comedians.

The Iron Giant

Great mix of childlike wonder and the idea of nature vs nurture. Emotionally potent.

Wallace and Gromet: The Curse of the Were-Rabbit

Such cool claymation! WHY IS NO ONE DOING CLAY ANYMORE! Really funny, interesting little mystery with lots of neat animation sequences and good ol' fashion Brittish humor.
